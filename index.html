Mental models are "the big ideas from the big disciplines: physics, biology, psychology, philosophy, literature, sociology, history, and a few others." These foundational concepts help to explain the world around us and make us better thinkers.

This list is compiled from Tren Griffin's list of models here. Each model is described with a Wikipedia introduction level of specificityâ€”indeed, most definitions will reference Wikipedia unless otherwise stated. I will then note any additional relevant thoughts around each model, as well as a musing or two on how it could be applicable beyond business and investing to knowledge work more broadly.

Please add your commentary! I would love for this to be a "living list" of concepts that benefit from multiple perspectives.
