About This Reference

Mental models are "the big ideas from the big disciplines: physics, biology, psychology, philosophy, literature, sociology, history, and a few others." These foundational concepts help to explain the world around us and make us better thinkers.

This list is compiled from Tren Griffin's list of models here.
